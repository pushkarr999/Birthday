Ishita aka babudii (pronounced ba-bu-diiiiiiii; born 9 April 2001) is the founder of Company Secratary Institute For Amazing Girls Like Ishita Pvt. Ltd. Shes the kind of girl who steals my fries and somehow still steals my heart every single day. Her laugh is my favorite soundtrack, and her smile? Lets just say it could end wars and start new galaxies. I swear, even when shes mad at me for something I probably did (but wont admit), shes still the most beautiful chaos Ive ever known.

Early life[change | change source]

Ishita, known by many adorable aliases such as Babudii, Shembdi, Cuttu, Sweetu, Sunshine, and Love, was born with a natural talent for spreading joy and stealing hearts (and maybe a few fries along the way). From a young age, she had a gentle charm and a shy smile that could light up an entire classroom. She began her schooling journey in Army Schools across various corners of India, growing into a kind, thoughtful, and lowkey hilarious human being. But destiny truly hit its peak when she moved to Kamothe a moment now recognized in history as The Luckiest Day of Pushkar Sutars Life. Thats when the legend met her, and in a blink, he knew: she wasnt just the one she was the everything.

College Life[change | change source]

Ishita joined SIES to pursue her B.Com, but of course, she didnt stop therebecause why just do one thing when you can slay at two? Alongside college, she also began preparing for the prestigious Company Secretary exams (because brains + beauty is her default mode). She instantly became a college favorite, especially after setting the stage on fire with her iconic performance on Jhingaat. Some say the college never recovered from that much swag. Despite the fame, she stayed grounded and focused, cracking her CS Executive exams like a boss. Soon after, she joined ARCAS, where she didnt just fit inshe shined. Her work ethic, wit, and get-things-done attitude made her an irreplaceable part of the team. As of now, shes powering through her CS Finals with the same determination and grace (and occasional peri peri fries cravings) that make her who she is.
